# Logo

## Purpose
The Logo represents the LegacyHQ brand and provides a consistent brand identifier throughout the product.

## When to use
Use in application headers, authentication screens, marketing surfaces, and branded experiences.

## When not to use
Do not alter proportions, colors, spacing, or combine with other graphics outside approved variants.

## Design intent
Maintain consistent brand recognition while adapting to light and dark themes.

## Accessibility
Provide descriptive alternative text when the logo conveys brand identity. Decorative instances should be hidden from assistive technology.

## Related
Icon, Wordmark, Brand assets.
